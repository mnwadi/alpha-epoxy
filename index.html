Our Industrial Preparation Method

Adhesion is determined entirely by substrate profile. We do not use weak acid-etch kits—we execute diamond preparation steps to open concrete pores correctly.

Phase 01

Planetary Diamond Grinding

We deploy dual-rotation heavy diamond grinders equipped with vacuum extractors to remove surface laitance, oil spots, and aged sealer finishes, establishing a perfect mechanical profile tint.

Phase 02

Slab Leveling & Solid Priming

Structural crack segments and surface spalls are vacuum cleared and injected with polyurea compound. Next, we roll down a deep-penetrating 100% solid industrial base matrix resin.

Phase 03

Full-Rejection Flake Injection

While our premium base coating is structurally active, we manually throw hundreds of pounds of durable vinyl matrix flake chips, fully saturating the area to target absolute rejection depth.

Phase 04

Polyaspartic Clear Lock

After scraping away excess media, we apply our clear protective coat armor layer, sealing your custom flooring texture for quick vehicle return capabilities within 24 hours.

Rapid Return

Walk on your floor within a few hours; return your heavy vehicles to service in just 24–48 hours max.

Most typical 2-to-3 car residential garages are completely prepared, repaired, coated, and sealed within 1 to 2 consecutive days, depending entirely on necessary layout repairs.

Our full-flake broadcasting systems inherently possess a highly structured, slip-resistant surface profile texture. For high-gloss or metallic options, we can add specialized micro-media aggregate agents to boost grip security.

Acid washing only cleans superficial layers without curing surface laitance. Mechanical diamond grinding slices cleanly through deep layers, expanding structural concrete pores to yield a secure mechanical profile for deep resin lock-in.

You are free to walk safely across the floor surface within 8–12 hours. We strongly recommend waiting 24 to 48 hours before pulling heavy vehicles or industrial equipment onto our advanced topcoat layers.
